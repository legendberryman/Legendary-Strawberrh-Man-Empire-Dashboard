📅 Daily Workflow
1
48h after upload: Go to "+ 48h Data" (top right). Enter the numbers from YouTube Studio. The app scores it and tells you what to do.
2
Read the score: 75+ = strong, leave it. 55–74 = monitor 7 days. 35–54 = change thumbnail. Under 35 = change thumbnail AND title.
3
72h after any change: Check CTR in Studio. Up = working. No change = try new title too.
4
7 days after upload: Update 7d data. If score 60+ → mark "Repost Candidate" in the tracker.
5
Before every 3rd/7th upload: Check Repost Queue. Pick #1 ranked video in the right category and post it.

🎯 48h Decision Rules
CTR above category avg✓ Thumbnail working
CTR below avg 20%+→ Change thumbnail NOW
AVD above category avg✓ Content is strong
AVD below avg 20%+→ Hook weak? Check first 5 sec
Avg% viewed 50%+Strong retention = repost candidate
Browse % 70%+Algorithm pushing it ✓
The One Rule: Bad CTR + good AVD = thumbnail problem.
Good CTR + bad AVD = content/hook problem.
Both bad = change thumbnail AND title.
🧘 When to Make a 1-Hour Version
AVD% of video lengthMust be ≥ 35%
CTR%Must be ≥ 4.5%
Video categoryHealing only
Decision timingAfter 7-day data confirmed
Why both metrics matter: High AVD% = people want to keep listening (loop works). High CTR = thumbnail/title works at scale — needed for a 1-hour video to get discovered by new viewers.
Revenue note: 1-hour RPM is 3–5× higher. These are the money engine. Don't skip them when a Healing video qualifies.
